Spirekeeper

Spirekeeper is an newPixelart 2D platformer made by Bleitz.
playing as a cursed child who can transform into a werewolf
you will be working you through the toughest enemies
in the top-bottom designed levels named spires
at the end of a spire you meet the spirekeeper.
after completing every spire,
you will gain access to an online competition mode,
where the first one who slays the spirekeeper wins

And every single detail about Spirekeeper
will be added to the gamepedia article.

This game is still before alpha.
Testversions aren't out jet.
Any adviseses are appreciated,
just share it
using our new community function.

It's planned to always update Spirekeeper,
and balance Spirekeeper every Month.

even launching the game
and everything you will post
in the new community function
will help us create the perfect game.
